WELCOME TO SAVE FOOD WASTE

Often food waste is emphasized as one of the great sins in connection with achieving a sustainable development.
According to the Danish Veterinary and Food Administration approximately 1,214,000 tons of food ends up in Danish trash cans each year. With this fact in mind, several companies within the food business have committed themselves to measure their food waste to reduce it by half before the end of 2030
